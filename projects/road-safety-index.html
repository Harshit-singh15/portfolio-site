Himachal Pradesh Road Safety Index & Forecasting

A composite risk index ranking Himachal Pradesh districts by accident severity and landslide exposure, with ML models forecasting safety trends.

View repository ↗

Overview

Road safety in hill states like Himachal Pradesh depends on more than accident counts alone — terrain risk factors such as landslide exposure compound the danger. This project combines accident severity data with landslide exposure data into a single composite Road Safety Index, then ranks every district using normalized scoring.

Beyond the static ranking, machine learning models forecast how district-level safety is trending, helping identify areas where risk is increasing.

Approach

  • Collected and cleaned district-level accident severity and landslide exposure datasets
  • Built a normalized composite scoring method to rank districts on a common Road Safety Index
  • Trained forecasting models to project district-level safety trends forward
  • Identified risk-prone districts for targeted attention
